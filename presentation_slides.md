# English AI Study App
## A Modern AI-Powered Language Learning Platform

---

# Introduction

* The **English AI Study App** is designed to provide an all-in-one ecosystem for Cambodian learners (EN ↔ KH).
* Language learning requires consistency, interaction, and motivation.
* Built with a beautiful, responsive UI using **Flutter** and powered by artificial intelligence.
* Deployed across multiple platforms (Android, iOS, Web).

---

# What is English AI Study?

* A comprehensive mobile application designed to help users master the English language.
* It combines traditional learning tools (vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation) with modern AI conversational practice.
* Acts as a **Personal AI Tutor** in your pocket, available 24/7.
* Tracks your daily progress, study goals, and motivates you with achievements and local push notifications.

---

# Key Features (1/2)

* 📚 **Interactive Vocabulary**: Learn daily words categorized by level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) and type (Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives).
* 🗣️ **Audio Pronunciation**: Listen to correct word pronunciations to improve speaking skills.
* 🇰🇭 **Real-time EN ↔ KH Translator**: Seamlessly translate phrases between English and Khmer.
* ⭐ **Favorites & Personalization**: Save words to your personal study list.

---

# Key Features (2/2)

* 🤖 **AI Chat Tutor**: Have real-time, natural conversations with an AI that understands context and helps correct your English.
* 🎯 **Daily Study Goals**: Built-in 30-minute daily study tracker with active day streaks.
* 🔔 **Local Push Notifications**: Receive study alerts and achievement reminders directly on your phone.
* 👤 **Profile & Progress Tracking**: Upload profile pictures, track your level, and monitor your language mastery over time.

---

# APIs & Technologies Used

The application leverages a robust, modern technology stack:

* **Firebase Authentication** - 
  Secure email/password login and user management.
  `https://firebase.google.com/docs/auth`
* **Cloud Firestore** - 
  Real-time NoSQL database to sync user profiles, favorites, and chat history.
  `https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore`
* **Gemini AI API** - 
  Google's advanced Large Language Model serving as the brain of the AI Tutor.
  `https://ai.google.dev/docs`
* **Hive** - 
  Blazing fast local storage for offline vocabulary data.
  `https://pub.dev/packages/hive`
* **Flutter Local Notifications** - 
  OS-level alerts for study reminders.
  `https://pub.dev/packages/flutter_local_notifications`

# Summary

* **English AI Study** bridges the gap between static dictionary apps and expensive human tutors.
* It offers a secure, scalable architecture utilizing Firebase and a custom backend.
* It provides a rich user experience with dark mode, beautiful UI/UX (Material 3), and cross-device syncing.
* Completely functional, from account creation to real-time AI conversation and goal tracking.
